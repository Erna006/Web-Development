Travel to Paris

Why Paris?

Paris is not just the capital of France, it is the world capital of art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Every corner of this city breathes history and romance. From the majestic Eiffel Tower to the cozy streets of Montmartre, Paris promises an unforgettable experience.

What do you need to know before your trip?

The best time to visit Paris is spring or autumn. In summer there can be a lot of tourists, but in winter it can be cold. Don't forget to learn a few French phrases!

Main attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre
  • Notre Dame
  • Arc de Triomphe
